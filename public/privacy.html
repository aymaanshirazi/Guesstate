Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 June 2026

Guesstate ("we", "the game") is a free browser-based geography game. We built it to respect your privacy. This page explains, in plain language, exactly what data is and isn't involved.

The short version

We do not collect your name, email, account, or any personal profile. We have no login and no user database. Payments are handled entirely by Ko-fi — we never see your card details.

What is processed when you play

  • Anonymous analytics (Cloudflare Web Analytics). We use a privacy-friendly, cookieless analytics tool to see aggregate stats like page views, approximate country, and browser type. It does not use cookies and does not build a profile of you or track you across sites. See Cloudflare Web Analytics.
  • Local storage on your device. If you unlock Cities Mode, we store a single flag (guesstate_pro_v1) in your browser so you stay unlocked. This stays on your device and is never sent to us. You can clear it any time via your browser settings.
  • Web fonts (Google Fonts). The game loads fonts from Google's servers, which means your IP address is processed by Google to deliver them. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Purchases (Ko-fi). If you buy the Cities Mode unlock or tip us, that transaction happens on Ko-fi, under their privacy policy. We only receive what Ko-fi shows sellers (e.g. that a sale happened); we do not receive or store your payment details.

What we do NOT do

  • No accounts, logins, names, or emails collected by the game.
  • No tracking or advertising cookies.
  • No selling or sharing of personal data (we don't have any to sell).
  • Your guesses are gameplay, not personal data.

Your rights

Because we don't hold personal data tied to your identity, there is little to request. If you have a question or a privacy concern (including GDPR/CCPA requests), contact us via our Ko-fi page and we'll help.

Children

Guesstate is a general-audience game and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes

We may update this policy as the game evolves. The "last updated" date above will change accordingly.

Contact

Questions? Reach us through our Ko-fi page.
